Here is some homemade chicken parm, fan favorite and will win over the hearts of your friends by having delicious carbs and tons of cheese.

Requirements:

  • Something to fry chicken(pot w/ oil works)
  • Kitchen Torch(extra step, for cheese on top)

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Egg yolk
  • Italian Seasoning, Bread Crumbs, Italian Breading
  • Angel Hair pasta
  • Parmesan, freshly grated
  • Mozzarella
  • Homemade marinara(seperate recipe, too lazy too add in this)

Steps:

  1. Make marinara few hours in advance
  2. Slice chicken breast vertically
  3. Wet the chicken in egg wash(scrambled egg and set aside in bowl to dip chicken)
  4. Put chicken in breading mix(Italian Seasoning, Bread Crumbs, Breading, Parmesan)
  5. Cook angel hair pasta
  6. Take breaded chicken and fry until cooked completely
  7. Plate in order: Pasta, Chicken, Marinara, Mozzarella
  8. Take kitchen torch and fry the cheese on top
