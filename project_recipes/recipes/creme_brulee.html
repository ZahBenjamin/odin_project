A recipe so good your friends and family will always ask you to make it no matter how much you are tired of making it. This picure is before the brulee, to show the consistency and texure.

Requirements:

  • Ramekins
  • Kitchen Torch

Ingredients:

  • 16 eggs
  • 7 liquid oz, vanilla extract
  • 1 quart heavy cream
  • 14 oz sugar

Steps:

  1. seperate egg whites into bowl
  2. add sugar and vanilla extract
  3. whisk together until combined
  4. heat heavy cream, steep it
  5. while constantly mixing egg mix, add cream in at a ladle full at a time
  6. when egg mix is at a higher temp, can put rest of cream in at once
  7. air bubbles are at the top of the mixture, lightly burn them away with kitchen torch
  8. put ramekins on tray, with enough height to add water between
  9. pour mixture in and bake at 250
  10. set a timer and check every 10 minutes, until creme is set and no longer liquid
  11. take out of oven and cool in fridge
  12. when cool, pour spoon of sugar over top and lightly burn with kitchen torch to brulee
